Geometry Calculator

Cube

Formula
Volume = side³
Surface Area = 6 * side²

Sphere

Formula
Volume = 4/3 * π * radius³
Surface Area = 4 * π * radius²

Cylinder

Formula
Volume = π * radius² * height
Surface Area = 2 * π * radius * (radius + height)

Cone

Formula
Volume = 1/3 * π * radius² * height
Surface Area = π * radius * (radius + slantHeight)

Rectangular Prism

Formula
Volume = length * width * height
Surface Area = 2 * (length * width + width * height + height * length)

Pyramid

Formula
Volume = 1/3 * base² * height
Surface Area = base² + 2 * base * slant height

Tetrahedron

Formula
Volume = (edge³) / (6 * √2)

Hemisphere

Formula
Volume = 2/3 * π * radius³
Surface Area = 3 * π * radius²

Torus

Formula
Volume = 2 * π² * radius * tube radius²
Surface Area = 4 * π² * radius * tube radius
